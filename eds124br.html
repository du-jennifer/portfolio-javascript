Teaching Computational Thinking

Other than enjoying computing myself, I find the need to encourage the younger generation, especially under represented populations in STEM, to pursue computing by entrusting my knowledge to them. To better accomplish my goal, I took a course at UCSD that examines computing education, both educational issues and computing tools. Through this course, I am able to stay well-informed and emphathetic while developing teaching and accessment methods through novice programming frameworks for beginning programmers.
